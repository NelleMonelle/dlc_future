How to use your enemy tension bar:
(Given to you by Lancer Industries)

Tension Bar variable: Game.battle.enemy_tension_bar
(Uses the same functions as a normal tension bar, a.k.a: show(), hide(), getTension(), removeTension(amount), giveTension(amount, dont_clap), etc...)

Encounter initial variables:
(Can be set in the encounter inital function)

self.enemy_tension_bar_on_start    |  Whether the tension bar will enter the screen when the battle starts



By default, the enemy will gain TP when you graze, can be disabled in the mod.json.
